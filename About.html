Use DIY CHIC to help you with your renovation or DIY projects. Use the Inventory Tracker to create a list of your of tools, hardware or even record household items such as furnace filters, lightbulb wattage or more. The Shopping List option allows you to create a list of all the materials that you require for your next project. With DIY CHIC you'll be prepared for your next visit to Home Depot, RONA, Lowes or Canadian Tire and will avoid wasting time making extra trips to the returns counter.

DIY CHIC also includes several handy calculators to help you organize the materials for your next project. Stay current of DIY tips and get new project ideas to make your project easier. Another key feature is the listing of garbage and recycling schedules for major cities in both Canada and the USA, which can be used to help stay tidy and organized during your projects.
